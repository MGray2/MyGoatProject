About

Richard Jordan Gatling (September 12, 1818 - February 26, 1903) was an American inventor best known for his invention of the Gatling gun, which is considered to be the first successful machine gun.

What He Is Great At

While being most known for inventing the Gatling gun, Gatling invented and patented a number of other inventions. His inventions include a screw propeller and a wheat drill in 1839, a hemp break machine in 1850, a steam plow in 1857, the Gatling gun in 1861, a marine steam ram in 1862, and a motor-driven plow.
